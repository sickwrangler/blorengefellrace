Route

Welcome to a run that's a cut above your average park stroll! The Blorenge Challenge takes you up the mountain's most formidable face, offering a thrilling test for runners of all levels. While it's a serious challenge that will push your limits, we believe in the potential of every participant to conquer this epic course.

Navigation is a key part of the adventure. Instead of flags and marshals guiding your way, you'll rely on your own skills and tools. You must carry a bringing a map and we recommend a compass. It's all part of the excitement and personal growth that comes with fell running!

Below, you'll find a map showing the most common route. However, we encourage you to explore and find your own path - that's the beauty of fell running! Consider a preparatory visit to familiarise yourself with the terrain and potentially discover your own unique approach to the challenge.
