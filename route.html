Route / Llwybr

Croeso to a run that's a cut above your average parkrun! The Blorenge fell race takes you up the mountain's steepest face, offering a thrilling test for runners of all levels. While it's a serious challenge that will push your limits, we believe in the potential of every participant to conquer this epic course.

The course offers an authentic fell running experience with its mix of technical ascents and thrilling descents, making it an exciting yet accessible challenge for those looking to test their limits while enjoying the beauty of the mountain landscape.

Below, you'll find a map showing the most common route. However, we encourage you to explore and find your own path - that's the beauty of fell running! Consider a preparatory visit to familiarise yourself with the terrain and potentially discover your own unique approach to the challenge.
